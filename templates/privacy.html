Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Basic usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To display your expense history and generate summaries.
  • To diagnose errors and improve app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using industry-standard algorithms before being saved. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third-party services. We may use minimal infrastructure services (such as hosting providers) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly.
